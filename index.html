Journal Paper
2024

ELT: Easy Label Trainer

David Nyarko, Kelechi Nwachukwu, Anjolie Anthony, Kofi Nyarko

Springer Nature, Lecture Notes in Networks and Systems

This paper presents ELT-Easy Label Trainer (ELT), a tool designed to automate and streamline the process of labeling and training object detection models. It integrates advanced models like YOLOv8, YOLOv9, and YOLO-World for robust object detection and segmentation using descriptive text prompts.
